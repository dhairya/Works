title: tweet-a-fleur
-
date: 2010 
-
content: 
tweet-a-fleur is a real physical flower that sits on your desk. The flower senses the happiness quotient of the world by affectively analyzing real-time public twitter feeds. Based on the happy / sad state of the world - the flower physically blooms / droops. 

<br />

<b>role:</b> Conceptualization, Hardware and Software Development. 

<b>skills:</b> Arduino, Shape-memory alloy, Processing, EmoTweet-core, Twitter API. 

-
keywords: A physical flower that embodies the happiness quotient of the world.
