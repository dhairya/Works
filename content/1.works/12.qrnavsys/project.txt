title: QRNavSys
-

-
content: 

A QR code based urban information and indoor navigation system makes for an ideal alternative to GPS in developing countries like India where a sizable amount of cell phones are low-end camera equipped. QR-NavSys explores user scenarios and experience in a setting of Mumbai, India. 

<br />

<b>role:</b> Conceptualization, User Research. 

<b>skills:</b> Story boarding.
