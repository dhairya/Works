content: 
Fiber-optic cables have the property of letting light pass without distortion even when bent. inVisible uses this property to construct a fiber-optic mesh which renders the object underneath invisible. 

<br />

<b>role:</b> Conceptualization, Apparatus Development. 

<b>skills:</b> Optics, Rapid prototyping.
