title: iFeel

-
content: 
We share this planet with billions of machines. Imagine if they had feelings - how they would make sense of the world. iFeel is a case study in form of a short-film of how would it be to lead a life of a smartphone. 

<br />

<b>role:</b> Conceptualization, User research, Direction. 

<b>skills:</b> iOS SDK, Story boarding, Interviews, Video editing and production.
