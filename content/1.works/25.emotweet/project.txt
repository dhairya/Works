title: EmoTweet
-
date: 2008
-
content: 
EmoTweet sources live tweets, senses emotions and creatively visualizes them. A combination of six basic emotions- happiness, anger, fear, surprise, sadness, and disgust at various scales make up a final visualization of the tweet Textual emotion recognition is based on the lexicon of emotion-related words, where each word is associated with a vector of weights for each emotion type and by several simple heuristics (usage of upper cases, exclamation mark, emoticons etc.). 

<br />

<b>role:</b> Conceptualization, Software Development, Visualizations. 

<b>skills:</b> Processing, Synesketch API, Twitter API.
-
keywords: Affective analysis and visualization of tweets.
