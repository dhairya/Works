404

This is the unknown land,
where no man has even trod.
Where lay the puzzles and mysteries,
that may take humanity ahead.
Inch by inch,
byte by byte.
I am here right now,
thinking, building, reflecting.
This may be the space,
where my next discovery lie.
Or just another unknown,
waiting for its Columbus.
You have two choices,
find your unknown
or go back to the known.
